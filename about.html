About Prefab Innovations

Committed to solving Ethiopia’s housing crisis with modern, sustainable construction.

Our Commitment

Prefab Innovations is dedicated to introducing affordable, sustainable prefabricated wall panel technology that accelerates building without compromising quality or durability.

Vision Statement

We envision becoming a leader in prefab housing construction where every Ethiopian has access to safe, affordable housing with minimal environmental impact.

Mission Statement

To revolutionize construction across Ethiopia through innovation, education, and sustainable materials, providing quality, safe, economical, and fast home building products and services.

Our Approach

  • Using precast panels to reduce construction time and costs
  • Training local workers and fostering entrepreneurship
  • Partnering with local governments and NGOs
